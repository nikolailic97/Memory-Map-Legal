Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Memory Map ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how the Memory Map mobile application collects, uses, and protects information.

Information We Collect

User Content

Memory Map allows users to create personal memories including:

  • Titles and descriptions
  • Photos selected by the user
  • Emotions and tags
  • Location data associated with memories
  • Dates and timestamps

This content is created and controlled entirely by the user.

Local Data Storage

All memories and related data are stored locally on the user's device. Memory Map does not upload or store your memories on external servers.

Location Information

Memory Map may request access to your device location in order to allow you to attach locations to your memories and display them on the map. Location access is optional and can be disabled in your device settings.

Photos and Media

The app may request access to your device's photo library so that you can attach photos to memories. Photos remain stored on your device unless you choose to export or share them.

Notifications

Memory Map may send optional notifications such as daily reminders, weekly reflections, or memory streak notifications. Notifications can be disabled at any time through your device settings.

In-App Purchases

Memory Map offers optional premium features through in-app purchases. Payments and subscription processing are handled by the Apple App Store or Google Play Store and managed through RevenueCat.

Memory Map does not store or process payment information directly.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services including:

  • Google Maps (for map display)
  • RevenueCat (for subscription management)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store (for payments)

These services may collect limited technical information required for their functionality.

Data Export and Backup

Memory Map allows users to export memories as files (such as PDF or backup files). These exports are created locally on the user's device and are controlled entirely by the user.

Children's Privacy

Memory Map is not directed toward children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
